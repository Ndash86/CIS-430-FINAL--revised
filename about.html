About The Hidden Tower

 

The Hidden Tower Restaurant is owned by their parent corporation ABC INC. ABC is a leading corporation within the United States and abroad with arguably the most recognizable and successful restaurant chains. A part of the corporation’s success is due to the constant publicity ABC has received from its hit television shows 'Hell’s Diner’ & ‘Steel Chef.’

 

Recently, the corporation has expressed an interest in expanding its reach and has come to the decision that a new restaurant will be built within the City of San Francisco. San Francisco was chosen due to its unique position as a city. San Francisco is an attraction for tourists, a major cultural hub, and is home to many technological firm headquarters. The theme of the restaurant will be high-end focused, offering fine dining and quality foods, all the while maintaining a casual atmosphere.
